Carter Creek

3514 Carter Creek

Property Description

Nestled in the heart of Bryan, this meticulously maintained 4-bedroom, 2-bathroom home is a perfect blend of comfort and convenience. Boasting a spacious living and dining area combination, the home also features a 2-car garage. Step outside to discover a screened-in porch, ideal for year-round enjoyment. The backyard is a true retreat, complete with an inviting inground pool, a large expanse of green space, and additional storage sheds. The property sits on a sizable lot with a sprinkler system and is fully fenced, providing both privacy and security. Enjoy outdoor gatherings on the patio or explore the nearby Tanglewood Community Park, which offers picnic tables, a covered pavilion, tennis courts, a splash pad, open green fields, playground equipment, and more. The newly renovated, award-winning Sul Ross Elementary School, Bryan High School, shopping centers, grocery stores, and dining options are conveniently located within a mile radius, with Texas A&M University just a short 2.5-mile drive away. All leases participate in the Latchel Resident Benefit package. The additional fee for this package is $40 per month.

Contact us with any questions about renting, touring, or anything else by emailing max@lurieestates.com or by visiting our contact page.
